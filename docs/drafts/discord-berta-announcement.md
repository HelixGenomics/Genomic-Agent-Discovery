New Feature: Protein PRS Scoring

We just added **protein polygenic risk scoring (PRS)** to the Helix Sequencing pipeline — this uses BERTA (a protein language model) to score how genetic variants affect protein structure and function.

**What you'd do:**
1. Clone the repo and set up locally (takes ~10 min)

3. Share your results in #show-your-results (anonymized is fine)
4. Report any bugs in #bug-reports

**Why this matters:**
Traditional PRS only looks at statistical associations. Protein PRS uses AI to predict how your specific variants change protein folding and function — it's a fundamentally different approach.
