## Where to Publish Mark's Case

**Best fit journals:**
- **Orphanet Journal of Rare Diseases** — open access, IF ~4.0, specifically for rare diseases
- **Molecular Cytogenetics** — specialized in chromosome abnormalities, open access
- **American Journal of Medical Genetics Part A** — premier clinical genetics journal
- **BMC Medical Genomics** — open access, specifically for genomic case studies
- **medRxiv** (preprint) — post while submitting to journal, establishes priority

**Follow CARE guidelines:** https://www.care-statement.org/ — 13-item checklist for case reports
